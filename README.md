### 🧑‍💻 About Me

Software Engineer based in **Phnom Penh, Cambodia** with professional experience building enterprise applications, manufacturing systems, and business process automation solutions. I work across the full stack — from **React** and **Angular** frontends to **Laravel** and **NestJS** backends, including **Electron** desktop apps and **Flutter** mobile apps.

- 🔭 Currently working at **Yorkmars Garment MFG Co., LTD** as a Software Developer
- 🌱 Building with **Laravel 12**, **React 19**, **Angular 19**, **NestJS 11**, and **TypeScript**
- 💼 Experienced in real-time systems (Socket.io, Pusher), QR/barcode scanning, and PDF/Excel reporting
- 🎓 Bachelor of Computer Science from **CADT University** (2021–2024)
